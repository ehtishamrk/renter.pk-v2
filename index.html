Our Story

About Renter.pk

Changing how Gujranwala rents — one listing at a time

Building Gujranwala's Rental Revolution

Founded in 2024, Renter.pk was born from a simple observation: finding quality rentals in Pakistan was unnecessarily difficult, fragmented, and untrustworthy. Whether it was apartments, vehicles, tools, or designer clothes — the process was broken.

We set out to fix that. Starting from Gujranwala, Renter.pk is Pakistan's first trust-backed rental marketplace, connecting renters with verified listings — secured by escrow and backed by identity verification.

Our Values

Trust & Security

Every listing is verified, every transaction is escrow-protected, and every user is CNIC-accountable.

Fair for Both

We protect renters from fraud AND owners from non-payment. Both sides win on Renter.pk.

Community First

We're rooted in Gujranwala. Local community, local focus, local trust — before we scale.

1. Introduction

Renter.pk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect: name, email, phone number, postal address, payment information (processed securely), government-issued ID (CNIC) for verification, and profile information.

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our services, we collect: device information, IP address, usage data, and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process transactions and send related information
  • To verify user identity and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. CNIC documents are stored encrypted in a private, access-controlled vault. Full rental addresses are hidden until a booking is confirmed and paid.
